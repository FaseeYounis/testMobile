About Me

Whenever I think of the word ‘technology’ I always associate it with the future. I believe that in our current generation technology has been a great asset in the way we learn, the way we teach, and in the way we go about our daily life. There has been an extraordinary number of advances in technology in the past few decades and there are no signs of this slowing down. The idea of virtual reality over 50 years ago would feel like a dream, yet here we are with such a creation, to be in our world and experiencing another is an amazing notion.

Work Experience

This required me to rapidly understand how to read and analyse financial statements and produce succinct reports. My brief was to help understand the drivers of performance for an NHS hospital. Furthermore, I was asked to prepare for client presentation. I had quickly come up to speed with the client’s situation and the team’s recommendations. I was responsible for managing my own time, priorities and delivering high quality work.
